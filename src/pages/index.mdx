Hi! My name is Ishan Deshpande, and I am a rising sophomore pursuing a degree in ECE at UT Austin, with a focus in Computer Architecture. I’m interested in working as a full-stack robotics engineer, creating everything from the circuit board to the code. During my freshman year, I worked as an Undergraduate Research Assistant at the Rewire Robotics Laboratory. I’m also a member of the Longhorn Racing Solar Team, where I work with the controls team on embedded systems used to control a solar powered vehicle. Over the summer, I am an intern at EnergyX, powering the future with efficient lithium extraction. In my free time, I like to tinker with hobby robotics and listen to/produce music.

Through this portfolio, I hope to give you a more in-depth look at my experiences and career as a whole, whether that be coursework, professional experiences, or personal projects.
